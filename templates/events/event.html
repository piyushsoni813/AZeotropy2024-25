ChemVision Lecture Series

Prof.Rakesh Aggarwal

Immerse yourself into lecture series "ChemVision" by AZeotropy. The first speaker in this session is Prof. Rakesh Agrawal. Prof. Rakesh Agrawal is Winthrop E. Stone Distinguished Professor in the Davidson School of Chemical Engineering at Purdue University, Indiana, USA.


He will discuss on an interesting topic "A Sustainable Energy Future – A Case for a Solar Economy" where he will discuss the transition to a sustainable solar economy. Explore challenges, interdisciplinary solutions, and the exciting potential of solar cells. Embrace the journey towards a renewable future.


ChemVision by Prof. sangte kim

Immerse yourself into the lecture series "ChemVision" by AZeotropy. The next speaker in this series is Prof. Sangtae Kim. He is the Jay and Cynthia Ihlenfeld Head (2016-present) and distinguished Professor of Chemical Engineering at Purdue University, Indiana, USA. Discover the journey of translational research from university labs to real-world impact, as Prof. Kim shares personal experiences with deliberate innovation strategies. Learn how to navigate the "valley of death" and bridge academia with commercialization for societal benefit.


Don't miss out on this opportunity to explore the application of the "trillion-dollar equation" to entrepreneurial teams, alongside exciting R&D projects like transforming shale gases and designing small molecule therapeutics.


ChemVision by Dr. Asit Kumar Das

Excited for the next ChemVision lecture by AZeotropy! Dr. Asit Kumar Das, head of R&D of the world's largest refinery at Jamnagar, Reliance Industries Limited, the brilliant mind behind over 100 national/international patents and 80 impactful publications, is taking the stage!


He'll delve into the captivating theme "Developed India: An Image of India’s Future in Energy & Chemicals" where he will discuss about India's path towards becoming a leader in Energy & Chemicals, aiming for self-sufficiency and sustainability. With a booming economy and ambitious goals, India is poised to drive significant growth in the energy and chemical sectors, embracing renewable energy and innovative technologies. Join the journey towards sustainable development and innovation in the chemical industry.


ChemVision by Prof. Abraham Stroock

Excited for the next ChemVision lecture by AZeotropy! Prof. Abraham Stroock, distinguished professor at Cornell University, renowned for his pioneering work in chemical and biomolecular engineering. With a background spanning physics and chemical physics, his research at the Stroock lab focuses on understanding and manipulating dynamic processes at micrometer scales, particularly in the context of plant physiology and synthetic systems inspired by plant functions


He'll delve into the captivating topic "The lungs of our planet - engineering analyses plant leaf function" he will discusses how plants manage exchanges between Earth and the atmosphere, focusing on the balance between carbon assimilation and water transpiration. He introduces AquaDust, a nanotechnology from his lab, revealing insights into leaf transport processes and a mechanism enhancing water-use efficiency.


Panel Discussions

Chemical Industry 5.0

We bring you the most awaited panel discussion on the topic "Chemical Industry 4.0: Smart Tech Revolutionizing Efficiency and Sustainability" where you will gain insights about the increasing importance of sustainability in energy, a need for change, how chemical engineers can contribute to it, what research is being conducted and how it resonates with the the industry, and how new technologies like AI/ML, AR/VR etc are being integrated in industries!


Joining the panel are Dr. Kishor Dongaonkar, Vice Precident, Reliance Industries; Mithu Saha, Energy Transition Manager, Worley; Subhash Dhar, President (Technology), Deepak Nitrite Limited; Prof. Mani Bhushan, Professor at IIT Bombay who have a varied and high experience in the chemical industry.
